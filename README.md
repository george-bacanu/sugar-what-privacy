# Privacy Policy for Sugar What

**Last Updated: December 11, 2025**

## Introduction

Sugar What ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Sugar intake logs**: Food/product names, sugar amounts, timestamps
- **Product library**: Custom saved products (premium feature)
- **User preferences**: Daily goals, visual unit preferences, theme settings

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Camera access**: Used solely for barcode scanning, no images are stored
- **Device information**: App version, device type (for crash reporting if enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Track and display your daily sugar intake
- Calculate weekly summaries and statistics
- Store your custom product library (premium users)
- Scan barcodes to retrieve nutritional information
- Sync your preferences across app sessions

## Data Storage

- **Local storage only**: All your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
- **No cloud sync**: We do not upload your personal data to any servers
- **No account required**: The app works completely offline

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### RevenueCat (Premium Features)
- **Purpose**: Process in-app purchases
- **Data collected**: Purchase receipts, anonymized user ID
- **Privacy policy**: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Barcode API (Optional)
- **Purpose**: Retrieve nutritional information from barcodes
- **Data collected**: Barcode numbers only (no personal information)
- **Data sent**: Only when you scan a product

## Camera Permissions

- Camera access is used exclusively for scanning product barcodes
- No photos or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted
- You can revoke camera permission at any time in device settings

## Children's Privacy

Sugar What is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access your data**: All data is visible within the app
- **Delete your data**: Use "Reset App Data" in Settings to delete all data
- **Opt-out**: Simply uninstall the app to remove all data

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device with encryption
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- Your data remains private and under your control

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
